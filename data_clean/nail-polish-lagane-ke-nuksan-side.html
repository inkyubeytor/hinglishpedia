Ladies khoobsurat dikhne ke liye nail polish ka istemal jaroor hi karti hain. Lekin kya aapko maloom hain ki beauty product ke roop mein pahchan bana chuki nail polish bhi khatarnaak saabit ho sakti hain. Janiye Nail polish mein kitne khatarnaak chemicals hote hain jo aapko nuksan pahucha sakte hain. Nail polish lagane ke nuksan (Side-effects of Nail Polish in Hindi/Urdu.) Darasal Nail polish mein mauzood chemicals eye aur skin ke contact mein aane par health par bahut bura asar daalte hain. Nail polish ya Kisi bhi dusre colourful beauty product ko banane ke liye Formaldehyde chemical ka istemal hota hain, Yeh chemical beauty product ko chipchipa banane ke liye istemal hota hain. Skin ke contact mein aane se aapko khujali ki problem ho jati hain. Isse allergy bhi ho sakti hain. Nail polish mein Phthalates naam ka ek oily chemical hota hain, Jo lagate waqt isme crack nahi padne deta hain. Lekin jab yeh aankho aur mooh ke contact mein aata hain to infection hone ka khatra badh jata hain. Iski wajah se naak aur gale ke infection hote hain. Nail polish mein Toluene chemical sabse khatarnaak chemical hota hain. Iska istemal sabhi chemical ko ek saath mix karne ke liye kiya jata hain. Toluene se dimaag ko sabse zyada nuksan hota hain. Zyada der tak toluene soonghne se brain cells mar jate hain. Toluene ka istemal deewar aur gaadiyo ko rangne wale paint mein bhi istemal kiya jata hain. Yahi wajah hain ki dewaar aur gaadiyo ko rangte waqt log mask pahante hain. Isliye nail polish bhi paint ki tarah hi khatarnaak hain to agli baar jab aap ise lagati hain to ise apni aankh, tavcha, naak aur mooh se door rakhe, ho sake to nail polish lagate waqt mask pahne, yeh ek accha upay hoga. Isse cancer se bante hain. ○ Reedh ki haddi par asar padta hain :- Neurotoxins aise zehreele chemical hote hain jo direct brain par asar daalte hain. Iske alawa yeh reedh ki haddi ko bhi nuksan pahuchate hain. Nervous system ko bhi nuksaan pahuchane ke liye yeh chemical jana jata hain. ○ Triphenyl phosphate hota hain :- Nail polish ke label par kanhi bhi Triphenyl phosphate ka zikr nahi kiya jata hain.
