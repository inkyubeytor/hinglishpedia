Indian Handset maker Company Intex ne apni Aqua series ke tahat naya smartphone Bharat mein launch kar diya hain. Company ka yeh Aqua Turbo Handset 4G hoga. Jiski Keemat 7,444 Rupaye hogi. Is phone ki battery back bhi kafi accha hain. 5 inch ki hain display  Intex Company ne apne is naye smartphone Aqua Turbo 4G mein Android ka operating system diya hain. Isme aapko Android ka latest Lollipop Os milega. Vahi is handset mein aapko 5 inch ki display milegi. Iske alawa agar is handset ke processor par gaur kare to isme aapko 1.0 GHz ka Quad-Core MediaTek MT6735 Processor milega. Vahi Aqua Turbo 4G mein aapko 1 GB ki RAM milegi. Agar storage capacity ki baat kare to isme 8 GB ki internal memory milegi aur 32 GB tak Micro-SD card ki suvidha milegi. 5 Megapixel ka rear Camera  Intex ne apne is naye handset Aqua Turbo 4G ko kafi stylish banaya hain. Iska behatrin look users ko attract karega. Isme aapko 5 Megapixel ka rear camera milega, Jisme LED flash hoga. Iske saath hi 2 Megapixel ka front camera bhi uplabadh hain. Agar Connectivity ki baat ki jaye to Aqua Turbo mein aapko 4G, 3G, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi aur GPS aadi ki suvidha milegi. Battery back-up mein yeh phone kafi jabardast hain. Isme aapko 3000 mAh ki battery milegi. Color variants ki baat kare to isme aapko White aur Black color milega. Full Specification of Intex Aqua Turbo 4G :-  Model Aqua Turbo 4G Sim Dual-SIM Display 5-inch (720 x 1280 pixels) IPS display Memory RAM 1GB, ROM 8GB, External Card – 32 Connectivity GPRS, EDGE, 4G, 3G,Wifi, Bluetooth, USB Camera Front- 2, Rear – 5MP with Flash OS Android OS, v5.1.1 (Lollipop) CPU Quad-core 1.0 GHz MediaTek MT6735 GPU Mali-T720 Battery 3000 mAh Price Rs. 7,444
