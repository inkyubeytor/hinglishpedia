Mood ko sahi karne wale food kya hain? In swaalo ka jawaab janne ke liye yeh post jaroor padhe. Agar aapka mood kharab hain ya phir aapka dimaag kuch sust aur slow sa ho raha ho toh aur aap chah kar bhi usay theek nahi kar pa rahe hain toh khane ki kuch cheeze aapke mood ko khushnuma bana sakti hain. Kuch researches ke mutabik Serotonin, Endorphin, Dopamine aur dusre Neurotransmitter Hormones hote hain jo Dil aur dimaag ko accha aur khush rakhte hain. Isliye agar aapka mood kharab ho gya ho toh in cheezo ko jaroor khaye, Inse bigda mood bhi sudhar jata hain. Happy rakhne ke liye yeh cheeze jaroor khaye. Aap ekdum fresh feel karenge toh jante hain kuch aise foods ke bare mein jo aapka mood badal sakte hain. . 1. Agar aapka dimaag soost hain toh black tea, green tea ya orange lemon tea lene se mood ekdum change ho jata hain. Ji haan Journal of Nutrition ki ek research ke mutabik is tarah ki chai mein ek tarah ka amino acid paya jata hain. Jo bigde mood ko khushnuma aur soost dimaag ko ekdum sharp bana deta hain. . 2. Strawberry Potassium ka bahut accha source hain, Jo ki tantrikaon ko uttejit karne mein madad karta hain. Yeh vitamins ka bhi bahut accha source hain. Ise khane se hamara mood accha hota hain. . 3. Agar aapka mood kharab hain to, thoda sa Shahad kha le. Darasal, Shahad se niyamit sugar ki matra milti rahti hain. Isme Kaempferol aur Quercetin aadi paye jate hain, Jo ki mind ko stress free rakhne ka kaam karte hain. . 4. Jyada matra mein Protein lene se bhi stress kam hota hain. Isliye jab aap jyada tension aur stress feel kare toh Macchli, Anda aur doodh se bani cheezo ka sewan kare. Isse sharir ko protein aur amino acid milte hain. Tyrosine ek special kism ka amino acid hain jo Dopamine, Norepinephrine aur Epinephrine aadi Neurotransmitter ke production ko badhata hain. In rasayano se sharirik alertness aur energy badhne mein madad milti hain. Isliye agar aap accha feel karna chahte hain toh protein se bhare khadya padarth, Jaise Macchali, Anda, Meat, Phaliya, Paneer, Doodh aur Dahi aadi ka sewan jaroor kare. . 5. Nariyal khane se heartbeat turant control mein aa jati hain.
