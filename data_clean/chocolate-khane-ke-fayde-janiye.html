Chocolate jyada khana accha nahi hota hain, Lekin thodi si chocolate khane se health ko big benefits aur fayde milte hain. Jab bhi chota baccha chocolate khata hain, toh hum yahi kahte hain ki chocolate health ko nuksaan pahucha sakta hain. Kyonki hame chocolate khane ke fayde ke baare mein pata hi nahi hota hain. Jab aap chocolate ke fayde ke baare mein jan lenge to yakin maniye aap bhi chocolate ke Fan ho jayenge. Chocolate khane se sehat ko hone wale fayde :-  • Blood pressure mein :- Chocolate khane se Blood pressure hamesha control mein rahta hain. Dark chocolate mein Flavonoids aur Antioxidants hote hain. Hafte mein 2 se 3 baar thodi-thodi chocolate khane se blood pressure control mein rahta hain. • Instant energy de :- Jin logo ko achanak chakkar aane ya B.P. low hone ki problem ho, Unhe apne paas hamesha chocolate ko rakhna chahiye. Jab bhi aapko weakness feel ho turant chocolate khayiye. Isse body ko instant energy milti hain. • Dard door bhagaaye :- Chocolate khane se body mein Endrophin hormone paida hota hain. Isliye chocolate ko khane se yeh ek natural pain killer ki tarah kaam karta hain. • Dil ke liye faydemand :- Chocolate mein Copper aur Potassium paye jate hain. Isliye ise khane se heart diseases door rahte hain. Chocolate se blood circulation behtar banta hain. Sath hi regularly chocolate khane se cholesterol bhi control mein rahta hain. • Mental Disorders ke liye :- Chocolate mein flavonoids hota hain. Flavonoid blood flow ko behtar banata hain. Isliye chocolate khane se mental cases mein fayda hota hain. • Khansi ka ilaj :- Chocolate khane se aap khansi ko khatm kar sakte hain. Chocolate Coco se taiyaar ki jati hain, aur coco mein Theobromine hota hain, Jo khansi ko door karne mein aapki help karta hain. • Mood sahi kare :- Chocolate mein mood ko sahi karne ka gun hota hain. Ise khane se tension se chhutkara milta hain. Jab bhi aapka mood kharab ho jaye to thodi si chocolate kha le, aap better feel karenge.
