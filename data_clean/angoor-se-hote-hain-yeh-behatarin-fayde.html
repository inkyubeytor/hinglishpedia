Aap bhi garmiyo mein Angoor jaroor hi khate honge. Angoor hamare aankho ki roshini ke liye bahut hi faydemand mani jati hain. Aap soch rahe honge ki gajar khane se bhi aankho ki roshini badhti hain, toh samajh lijiye gajar ki tarah angoor bhi aankho ke liye bahut hi faydemand hain. Ek research mein yeh baat saamne aayi hain ki Angoor mein Oxidative paya jata hain, jo hamari eyes ke liye faydemand hota hain. Expert ka kahna hain ki rojana angoor ko apni diet mein shamil karne se yeh oxidative stress ki mauzoodagi mein retina ko healthy rakhne mein help karta hain. Research se yeh pata chala hain ki daily grapes khane se stress kam hota hain aur aankho ki roshini bhi theek rahti hain. Aaiye jante hain Angoor khane se hame aur kya-kya labh hote hain.
