Agar aap non-vegetarian hain aur fish khana aapko sabse jyada pasand hain, toh Rohu naam ki machhli ko aap acchi tarah se pahchante honge. Rohu machhali khane mein jitni tasty lagti hain, Utni hi paushtik bhi hoti hain. Rohu fish India ke alag-alag states jaise Odisha, Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, West Bengal aur Assam mein bade hi chaav ke saath khayi jati hain. Machhli ke saath-saath iske ando ko bhi tal kar khaya jata hain. Health ko behatar banane ke liye machali ka sewan karna bahut hi jaroori hain. Rohu machali khane se sharir ko kai saare swasthya labh hote hain. Isliye apni diet mein Rohu Fish ko jaroor shamil karna chahiye. Rohu Machhali khane se kya kya fayde aur benefits hote hain, Aaiye inke baare mein jante hain. Dimaag banaye tez :- Machhali aur dimaag ka ek accha mel dekha gya hain. Agar aap niyamit roop se Rohu machhali ko apne bhojan mein shamil karenge toh aapka dimaag majboot banega. Kam Fat wali machhali :- Rohu Fish mein Fat bahut kam hota hain. Ise kha kar aapko shakti milegi par Fat nahi. Dil ke liye faydemand :- Machhali mein Omega3 Fatty Acid hota hain Jo dil ko healthy banane ke kaam aata hain. Skin ki dekhbhaal :- Machhali khane se jhurriya der se padti hain, Jisse umar kam lagne lagti hain. Iske saath hi suraj ki dhoop se hone wale nuksaan se bhi rahat milti hain. Protein se bhari :- Machhali mein kafi saara protein paya jata hain aur Rohu machhali mein aur bhi jyada protein hota hain. Rohu machhali ko baccho aur bado sabhi ko protein ke liye khana hi chahiye. Cancer hone se roke :- Logo ko aajkal Cancer jaisi janlewa bimariya hone lagi hain, Jiska ilaj rohu machhali ke paas hain. Mana jata hain ki Rohu machhali mein Anti-Oxidant kafi acchi matra mein paye jate hain jo ki cancer se ladne mein madadgar hota hain. Vitamin C se bharpoor :- Rohu macchali mein kafi saara Vitamin C hota hain, Jisse health se related bimariya door hoti hain. Cough Sardi aadi rohu khane se door ho jate hain. Minerals ka khazana :- Iron, Zinc, Iodine, Potassium,Calcium aur Selenium aadi kuch aise minerals hain, Jo sharir ko rohu machhali ko khane se mil sakte hain.
