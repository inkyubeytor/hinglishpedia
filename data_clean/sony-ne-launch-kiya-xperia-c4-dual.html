Smartphone makers companies mein se ek Sony ne India mein apna Xperia C4 Dual phone launch kiya hain. Company ki aor se iski keemat 29,490 Rupaye batai gyi hain. Iske saath hi company ne is baat ki goshna ki hain ki Selfie-focused smartphone June se bazaar mein available hoga. Usse pahle Company ne Xperia M4 Aqua Dual smartphone ke May mein launch hone ki baat kahi thi.  Kuch aisi hain jankari  Bataya ja raha hain ki yeh Xperia C4 Smarphone Friday se White, Black aur Vibrant Mint Color mein Sony ke centers, Xperia Stores aur Major electronics stores par available hoga. Vahi phone ke specifications ke bare mein bataya gya hain ki yeh phone Android 5.0 Lollipop par run karega. Iske saath hi phone mein 5.5inch ka full HD (1080 x 1920 Pixel ) IPS display diya gya hain. Isko Backup dega Firm ka s Bravia Engine 2 aur Sports a Super Vivid mode. Phone ko power dega 64-bit 1.7 GHz Octa-Core (Cortex-A53) MediaTek MT 6752 processor. In sabhi suvidhaon ke saath phone par aapko mil raha hain Mail- T760 GPU aur 2 GB ki RAM. Camera par khas dhyaan  Ab baat kare agar camera ki to handset mein aapko milega 13 megapixel ka auto focus rear camera, vo bhi LED flash ke saath. Iske alawa Sony ka agla Selfie focused smartphone hone ke nate is Xperia C4 Dual phone par aapko milega 5 Megapixel ka front facing camera. Is camera par aapko milega Company ka Exmor R censor, LED flash aur 25mm Wide-angle lens ke saath.  Battery aur storage bhi hain jabardast  Is Xperia C4 dual mein par aapko milega 16 GB ki inbuilt storage. Iske baad aap ise 128 GB tak Micro SD ki madad se expand bhi kar sakte hain. Phone ko power de rahi hain 2600 mAh ki capacity wali battery. Company ki aor se is battery mein 12 hours 47 minutes ka talk time dene ka dawa kiya gya hain. Connectivity ke liye is par milega Bluetooth, Wi-Fi with Hotspot, 3G, 4G LTE, GPS / A-GPS, Glonass, Micro-USB, DLNA aur NFC ki suvidha.
