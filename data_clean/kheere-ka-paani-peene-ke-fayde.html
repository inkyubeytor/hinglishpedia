Ise subah uthate hi piyenge toh zyada asar karega. KEEP YOUR SKIN HEALTHY Dry aur itchy skin bhala kise acchi lagti hain? Iski wajah hain sun exposure, Air pollution aur Weather changes jinhe hum chah kar bhi avoid nahi kar sakte hain. Cucumber water pee kar hum apne skin ko healthy bana sakte hain. Isme vitamin B-5 acchi quantity mein hota hain aur yeh acchi glowing skin ke liye bhi jaroori hain. Dehydration se skin dry hoti hain aur kheere ka paani peene se hame dehydration nahi hoti hain.
