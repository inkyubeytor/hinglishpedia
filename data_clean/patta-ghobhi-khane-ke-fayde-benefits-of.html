Patta Gobhi jise hum Band Gobhi ke naam se bhi jante hain. Patta Gobhi (Band Gobhi) khane ke bahut hi fayde hain. Patta Gobhi (BandGobhi) Brassicaceae Family ki ek member hain Is family mein aur sabziya jaise Broccoli, Phool Gobhi aur Brussels sprout aate hain. Yeh sabziya badi aur phool ke aakar ki bade patto wali hoti hain. Jinki paidawaar ki shuruvat , Eastern Mediterranean aur Asia Minor mein mani jati hain. Yeh saal ke sabhi mahino mein  bazaar mein asaani ke saath mil jati hain aur ise healthy diet ka ek ahem hissa mana jata hain. Patta Gobhi kai rango ki aur kismo mein aati hain. Lal aur Hare rang ki patta Gobhi sabse jyada milti hain. Ise paka aur kachcha salad ke roop mein bhi khaya ja sakta hain. Patta Gobhi ka swaad halka sa meetha hota hain.  Isme Vitamin, Iron aur Potassium bharpoor matra mein hote hain. Ise Indian aur Western dono tarah ke food ko pakane mein istemal kiya jata hain. Diindolylmethane (DIM), Sinigrin, lepeol, sulforaphane aur  indole-3-carbinol, jaise labhdayak tatv hote hain. Yeh sabhi cancer se bachav karne mein sahayak hote hain. 2.
