Jyadatar log  sochte hain ki Alcohol sirf sharab ya beer aadi mein paya jata hain. Alcohol veh chemical hota hain jo nasha paida karta hain. Lekin aapko jaankar tazzub hoga ki aap apni zindagi mein kai aisi cheezo ka istemal karte hain, Jinme Alcohol paya jata hain. Aaj ki post mein hum kuch aisi hi cheezo  ke baare  mein  janenge, jisme Alcohol hota hain, ya phir jinhe  banane  ke liye alcohol ka istemal  kiya jata  hain.  Perfume :- Shaadi ya party mein khusboo se sabka dil jeetne  ke liye  aap  perfume ka istemal karte  hi honge. Lekin kya aapko pata hain ki in perfume ya Cologne mein 50 se lekar 90 percent tak alcohol paya jata hain. Is tarah ke perfume ka istemal dhyaan se karna chahiye.  Vanilla extract :- Sweets, Cake aur dusri meethe pakwano mein istemal kiya jane wala Vanilla satv (Extract) bhi alcohol se bhara  hua hota hain. Darasal isme paye jane wali alcohol ka level, Vodka  aur Gin  ke barabar  hota hain. Yahi wajah hain ki Vanilla satv  ki kuch boondo ka hi istemal  kiya jata hain.  Cough Syrup :- Cough, Khansi ya Seene ki jakdan ke liye  aap jis cough syrup yani ki khansi ki dawaa ka istemal karte hain, Darasal usay banane ke liye  kuch alcohol ka istemal kiya jata hain. Yahi wajah hain ki aksar khansi ki dawa peene ke baad  nasha aane ke saath hi kai baar neend bhi aa jati hain.  Mouthwash :- Mooh ko bacteria aur mooh ki badboo se bachane ke liye aap har subah mouthwash ka istemal karte hain, Usme lagbhag 30% tak Alcohol paya jata hain. Galti se bhi agar aap mouthwash nigal le toh yeh aap par nashila asar daalta hain.  Sanitizer :- Daily aap apne haatho ko saaf rakhne  ke liye jis Sanitizer  ka istemal  karte hain, usme lagbhag 60 se  85% tak alcohol paya jata hain, jo ki bacteria ki behtar safaai karne mein  helpful hoti hain. Halaki market mein  Alcohol free sanitize r bhi available hain.
