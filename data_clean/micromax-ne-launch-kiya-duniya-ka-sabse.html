Indian handset maker company Micromax ne Thursday ko apna naya smartphone Canvas Silver 5 ko launch kiya. Jiski Keemat 17,990 rupaye rakhi gyi hain aur Company ka yeh challenge hain ki yeh duniya (World) ka sabse patla (Slim) smartphone hain.  4.8 Inch hain display  Micromax company ne apne is naye smartphone Micromax Canvas Silver 5 mein Android Operation System diya hain. Isme aapko Android ka latest version Lollipop 5.0 milega. Vahi is handset mein aapko 4.8 inch ki display milegi. Iske alawa agar is handset ke processor par gaur kare to isme aapko 1.2 GHz ka Quad-Core 64-Bit QualComm Snapdragon 410 processor milega. Saath hi 2 GB ki RAM milegi. Vahi agar Storage Capacity ki baat kare to isme aapko 16 GB ki internal memory milegi. Isme Micro SD card ki suvidha nahi hain.  8 Megapixel ka rear camera  Company ne apne is naye handset Micromax Canvas silver 5 ko kafi stylish banaya hain. Iska behtarin look users ko attract kar sakta hain. Is smartphone ki chaudaai 5.1 mm hain. Vahi isme 8 MP ka rear camera milega, jisme LED flash hoga. Jabki front camera 5 Megapixel ka hoga. Agar connectivity ki baat ki jaye to isme aapko 4G, 3G, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth aur GPS aadi ki suvidha milegi. Iske alawa battery backup mein yeh behatrin hain. Isme aapko 2000 mAh ki battery milegi. Color Variants ki baat kare to , Isme aapko Black aur Gold Colour milega. Full Specification :-  Model Canvas Sliver 5 Sim Single SIM Display 4.8-inch (1280 x 720 pixels) IPS display Memory RAM 2GB, ROM 16GB, External Card – no Connectivity GPRS, EDGE, 4G,3G, Wifi, Bluetooth, USB Camera Front- 5, Rear – 8MP with Flash OS Android 5.0 CPU 1.2GHz quad-core processor GPU Adreno 305 Battery 2000 mAh Price Rs. 17,999
