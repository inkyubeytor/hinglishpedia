Isse fefde se lekar muscles aur cell badhiya kaam karte hain. 4. Positive attitude :- Job ke liye interview ho ya shaadi ke liye mooh dikhai. Khoon ki kami nahi hoti :- Kam log hi jante hain ki hasne se white blood cells aur oxygen ki jyada se jyada matra body mein pahuchati hain. Dusro ki health :- Yeh wakai kuch alag aur hatkar hain. Hans kar khud to health banai ja sakti hain , Lekin kya aapko maloom hain ki isse dusre ko bhi healthy rakha ja sakta hain.
