Hamare khan-paan mein bhi kai aise poshak tatv hote hain, Jinki hame rojmarra ke kaamo ke liye jaroorat hoti hain. Aaiye jante hain inke baare mein:- Vitamin A  Yeh swasth Aankhon ke alawa Baalon, Skin, Haddiyon aur Daanto ke liye jaroori hain. Yeh immunity ko badhata hain. Kami se :- Raat mein Kam dikhna, Sukhi Skin aur Kamjor haddiyan Source :- Soya Milk, Dairy Products, Gajar, Palak, Hari Matar, Tamatar ka Juice, Tarbooz, Chukandar aadi. Vitamin B Sharir mein Carbohydrate ko energy mein badalne ka kaam yeh Vitamin karta hain. Yeh nervous System ko normal banane aur pachan shakti ko majboot rakhta hain. Kami se :- Dhyaan na lagna, Bhookh ki kami, Thoda kaam karte hi thakavat. Source :- Kela, Phaliyan, Aaloo, Chukandar, Tarbooz, Saabut Anaaj. Vitamin C Sharir ke Parts ko ek dusre se jode rakhne mein upyogi.   Kami se :- Masoodo mein khoon aana, Ghaav jaldi na bharna. Source:- Khatte Phal, Tamatar, Aaloo, Hari Mirch, Hari pattedar sabziyan. Vitamin D  Calcium ko sokhne mein madadgar aur Sharir mein phosphorus ke level ko badhaata hain. Haddiya majboot aur swasth rakhta hain. Kami se :- Kamjor haddiyan Source :- Doodh, Dhoop, Sabut Anaaj. Vitamin E  Sharir mein Lal rakt Kano (Red Blood Cells) ko banaane ka kaam karta hain. Kami se :- Pet samandhit rog. Source :- Sabut Anaaj, Daale aur Sukhe mewe (Dry Fruits). Vitamin K Khoon ke thakke banane se rokne ka kaam is vitamin ka hain. kami se :- Naak se khoon aana, Antrik raktsrav (Androoni khoon ka behna) Source:- Hari Sabziya, Soyabean.
