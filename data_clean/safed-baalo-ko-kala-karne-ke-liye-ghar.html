Kya aap apne safed baalo ko kala karne ke liye Dye ya Chemical wale colour ka istemal karte hain. Aapko nahi maloom hain ki yeh aapke baalo ke liye kitne nuksandeh hote hain aur saath hi aapki skin aur heath ko nuksaan pahuchaate hain. Inse aapke baalo ke girne aur pakne yaha tak ki Ganjepan hone ki problems ho sakti hain. Aaj hum aapko aisa gharelu nuskha batane ja rahe hain, Jiska istemal karke aap Ghar par hi natural baalo ko kala karne wala desi herbal dye bana sakte hain. To chaliye jante hain kaise banaye herbal natural dye. Ingredients :- 1. Sookhi peesi Mehandi -20 Gram. 2. Coffee powder – 3 Gram 3. Kattha – 3 Gram. 4. Amla Churan :- 10 Gram. 5. Brahmi Churan :- 10 gram. 6. Nimub ka ras – 4 Chammach. 7. Dahi – 25 gram. 1 se 5 number wali sabhi cheezo ko koot pees kar mila lijiye. Jaroorat ke mutabik mila kar rakh le. Baad mein 40 gram is mixture powder mein 25 Gram Dahi aur 4 Chammach Nimbu ka ras mila kar baalo mein aadhe ghante tak lagaye. Phir baalo ko paani se dho le. Iske istemal se aapke baal kaale, ghane aur mulayam ho jate hain. Note :- Kai baar baal pahli baar se hi kaale nahi ho paate hain. Toh jab aap 3 se 4 baar iska istemal karenge tab jakar aapke baal natural roop se kaale dikhayi dene lagenge. Agar choti hi umar mein aapke baal jhadne ya pakne lage hain toh apni diet par dhyaan dijiye. Khane mein jaroori Minerals aur Folic Acid ko shamil kare. Ek aur Tip :- Kaale baalo ke liye ek tips aur hain, Kalaunji 50 Gram lekar tawey par khoob garm kare, Jab yeh jal jaye toh ise pees kar raakh bana lijiye. Ab ek kadhaai mein aadha Kilo Sarso ka tel garm kare phir is tel ko thanda karke usme Kalonji ki raakh mila le, Kapde se chhan kar ise sheeshi mein bhar le. Ab is tel ko baalo mein lagaye, Isse baal Kaale hone ke saath, baalo ka jhadna bhi rook jata hain aur ganjepan mein naye baal bhi ug jate hain.
