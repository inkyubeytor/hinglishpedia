Aap sochte honge ki hari doob ghaas (Green doob grass) kewal puja paath aadi mein istemal hota hain, Lekin kya aapko maaloom hain ki hari doob ki komal pattiyo ko kai saare rogo ke ilaj mein bhi istemal kiya ja sakta hain. Iski taazi pattiyo ko saabut khane ke ilawa iska juice banaya jata hain. Kadwe swaad wali yeh doob ulti, cough, kabz, pregnancy sambandhi samsyaon ko door karti hain. Anti-Septic guno wali doob ghaas jakham bharne mein bhi kargar hain. Aaiye jante hain Hari doob ghaas ke gharelu nuskhe aur uske fayde :- Khujal ka ilaj :- Garmiyo mein paseene ke chalte aur sardiyo mein dryness ki wajah se body mein khujali hona aam baat hain. 2 Chammach doob ke ras ko til ke 100 gram tel mein mila kar khujali wali jagah par lagane se khujali door hoti hain. Mirgi ka ilaj :- 10 Gram hari doob ka ras ka istemal subah-shaam karne se mirgi rog mein fayda hota hain. Swaad ke liye aap isme thoda sa Shahad mila sakte hain. Skin problems ka ilaj :- Hari doob ko Haldi ke saath mila kar pees le aur iska ghol bana kar lagane se khaj-khujali, daad aur skin problems mein fayda hota hain. Nakseer ka ilaj :- Garmiyo mein naak se khoon nikalne ki samsya jyadatar logo ko preshan karti hain, Ise band karne ke liye Hari doob ghaas behtar hoti hain. Ise jameen se nikaal kar paani se dho le. Phir pees kar iska ras nikaal le. Iss ras ki 2-2 boonde naak ke dono chhedo mein daalne se nakseer band ho jati hain. Gathiya ka ilaj :- Badhti umar mein kamjor haddiyo wale sharir mein gathiya hona aam samsya hain. Hari doob ko lekar iske patto ka ras banaye aur iss tel se dard wali jagah par maalish kare. Jald rahat milti hain. Phati Eidiyo ka ilaj :- Kabhi Calcium ki kami toh kabhi pollution ke chalte aidiya ke phatne ki problem aam si baat hain, Lekin hari doob ke pattiyo ka paste bana phati ediyo par lagane se kafi aaram milta hain. Jhurriyo ka ilaj :- Badhti umar ke saath chehre par nazar aane wali jhurriyo ko door karne ke liye log tamam tarah ki beauty products ka istemal karte hain. Lekin inse pahle ek baar hari doob ko ajma le. 40 Gram Hari doob ki jad ka kadha subah-shaam peene se chehre ki jhurriya asaani se mit jati hain. Jakham ka ilaj :- Ghaav ko jaldi bharne mein doob bahut hi asardaar ilaj hain. Doob ko pees kar pake huye fode par lagane se foda asaani se bina dard ke phoot jata hain.
