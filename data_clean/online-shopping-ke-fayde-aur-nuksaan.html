Aaj ka zamane mein har kisi ko online shopping karna bahut hi accha lagta hain. Aadmi ho ya aurat sabhi ki pasand ban gyi hain oline shopping. Is bhaag-daur bhari busy life mein aaj logo ke paas itna time nahi hain ki veh market mein jakar shopping kar sake. Yahi wajah hain ki aajkal online shopping ka trends badh raha hain. Online shopping ke iss option ki wajah se log ghar baithe-baithe internet ke zariye asaani se shopping kar lete hain aur veh bhi apne favorites brands ke products ki.  India mein E-Commerce ka market aur  badh aur bhi tezi ke saath grow kar rahe hain, Kai tarah ki online shopping websites apne customers ko loobhane mein lagi huyi hain. Kai companies aisi hain jo free mein products ki home-delivery bhi deti hain, Kuch companies  Big Sale, Festival Sale tamam tarah ke sale ke naam par grahako ko attract karne ki koshish karti hain. Lekin Online  Shopping karne ke Fayde aur Nuksaan dono hi hote hain, Aaiye jante hain Online Shopping karne ke fayde aur nuksaan ke baare mein.  Online Shopping karne ke Fayde :-  ► Online Products  ka price local market se sasta hota hain.  ► Online shopping mein aapko bazaar mein jakar logo ki bheed ka saamna nahi karna padta hain.  ► Online Shopping mein time waste nahi hota hain.  ► E-commerce kanhi se bhi asaani se internet ke  jariye kiya ja sakta hain.  ► Aapko Online shopping karne mein maza aata hain.  ► Kai products local stores par easy available nahi hote hain, toh vehi products online badi hi asaani ke saath aapko mil jate hain.  ► Online shopping se customers zyada satisfy hote hain.  ► Companies aapki privacy ka khayaal rakhti hain.  ► Agar aap hamesha  busy rahte hain aur  aapko market mein jaane ka waqt nahi mil paata ho toh aap Online shopping ka luft utha sakte hain.  ► Online website customer ko protection bhi deti hain.  Online shopping karne ke nuksaan :-  ◄ Online shopping mein products ki quality ka pata nahi chalta hain.  ◄ Isme dhokha  hone ki sambhavna rahti hain.  ◄ Kuch companies samaan ki delivery karne par shipping charges bhi leti hain.  ◄ Online shopping mein order dene par samaan kai baar bahut late aapke paas deliver hota hain.  ◄ Online shopping mein products ki delivery mein uske defective ya tootne ka bhi darr hota hain.  ◄ Online shopping kabhi kabhi mehangi pad jati hain, Kyonki kai saamaan local bazaar mein kam keemat mein bhi available hote hain.   ◄ Agar koi cheez pasand na aaye toh usay vaapis karne mein mushkilo ka saamna karna padta hain.  ◄ Kuch products  ki warranty bhi nahi milti hain.  ◄ Kuch website farzi hoti hain, Jisse customer unke jaal mein phas jata hain. ◄ Credit card, Debit card mein dhoka hone ki sambhavna rahti hain.  Online  Shopping karne  ki quick tips :- Agar aap Online shopping karne ja rahe hain toh oos products ka price local market mein check kare. Ho sakta hain jo aap khareedna chahte hain usme kuch naya aaya ho, Kya pata local market mein veh sasta bhi ho.  Online shopping hamesha jaane-maane  websites se hi karni chahiye, Ho sake toh Cash On Delivery  ka option ka istemal kare. Kyonki fake website  se  agar  aap online shopping karte hain toh yeh aapki bank account ko hack bhi kar sakte hain. Isliye famous website se hi online shopping kare.  Online  shopping aaj kal ki jaroorat  ban gya hain, Ab yeh dheere-dheere small cities mein bhi famous hota chala ja raha hain. Aane wali duniya Online Shopping par hi based hogi.
