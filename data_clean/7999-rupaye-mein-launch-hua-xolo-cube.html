Indian Handset maker company Xolo ne haal hi mein Xolo Cube naam se smartphone lauch kiya hain. 5.0 Android Lollipop par chalne wale is smartphone ki keemat 7,999 Rupaye bataya gya hain. Company ki aor se yeh smartphone White, Black aur Golden color mein utara gya hain. Aap in teeno mein se apna manchaha rang chun sakte hain.  Specifications hain kuch aise :-  Ab baat karte hain Phone ke specification ki to phone mein 5 inch ka display diya gya hain, jo ki deta hain aapko 1280 X 720 pixel ka High Definition resolution. Is smartphone mein IPS display par Five point ka touch input diya gya hain. Phone ko power de raha hain 1.3 GHz Quad-Core (MediaTek 6582 M) processor. Saath mein hain Mali 400 MP GPU. Inke alawa isme aapko milegi 1 GB ki RAM.  Lollipop ke saath aur kya hain  Phone mein latest Android 5.0 aka Lollipop OS diya gya hain. 8 GB ki internal storage ke saath is par aapko 32 GB tak Memory ko expand karne ki suvidha milegi. Baat camera ki kare to phone aapko dega 8 Megapixel ka Rear camera flash ke saath aur selfie click karne ke liye milega aapko 2 Mega pixel ka front camera. Phone mein Rear camera full HD video recording ke saath diya gya hain.  Connectivity par ek nazar  Iske alawa Xolo Cube par connectivity ke naam par 3G, GPS, Micro USB 2.0 port, Bluetooth 4.0, Wi-Fi aadi ki suvidha milegi. Phone ko chalane ke liye is par aapko milega 2100 mAh ki battery. Is battery par aapko 3G Network ke saath 11 hours ka talk time aur 466 hours ka standby time milega.  Full specification :-  Model Xolo Cube 5.0 Sim Dual SIM Display 5-inch (1280 x 720 pixels) HD OGS IPS display with ASAHI Dragontrail glass protection Memory 1GB RAM, 8GB internal memory, 32GB expandable memory with MicroSD Connectivity 3G, GPS,AGPS,Wi-Fi (b/g/n), microUSB 2.0 port, Bluetooth 4.0 Camera 8MP rear camera with LED flash and 2MP front-facing camera OS Android 5.0 (Lollipop) CPU 1.3 GHz quad-core MediaTek MT6582M processor GPU 500 MHz Mali-400 GPU Battery 2100 MAh Battery Price Rs 7,999
