Mehndi (Mehandi/Heena) lagana na kewal saulah shringaar mein se ek hain, balki ise shubh shagun ke taur par bhi dekha jata hain. Hamare desh mein koi bhi aisa mauka aur festival aisa nahi hain jo bina mehandi haatho mein rachaye pura na ho. Mehandi tabhi khubsoorat lagti hain jab uska rang gahra ho aur yeh sahi se rache. Mehandi ka ek khas gahra chatak lal rang hota hain, Jo haatho par bahut hi khubsoorat lagta hain. Kai baar aisa hota hain ki mehandi lagi toh bahut acchi tarah se hoti hain, Lekin sahi se na rachne ki wajah se khubsoorat design bhi khil kar nahi aa pata hain. Aise mein aap in upay aur tips ko apna kar khubsoorat mehandi racha sakti hain. Mehandi ka rang kaise gahra kare? Gharelu Nuskhe for Mehandi (Heena) bright colour in Hindi. Aaiye jante hain Mehandi ka rang chatak chadhane ke liye kya karna chahiye. Halaki pahle yeh aap acchi tarah se jaanch le ki aapne mehandi ka ghol acche se taiyaar kiya hain. In upay aur tips ko apnane se gahri rachegi mehandi aur aayega chatak gahra lal rang :- • Nimbu aur Chini ke ghol ke istemal se bhi Mehandi ka rang gahra chadhta hain. Darasal is ghol ko lagane se mehandi zyada der ke liye haatho mein chipki rahti hain aur uska rang gahra ho jata hain. • Mehndi lagane ke baad patient rakhna bahut hi jaroori hain. Kam se kam 5 se 6 ghanto ke liye mehndi ko haatho par racha rahne de. Isse mehndi ka rang gahra chadhta hain. • Frying Pan mein Laung ki kuch kaliyo ko daal kar haath par uska dhooyaan lena bhi ek kargar upay hain. Aisa karne se mehndi ka rang gahra ho jata hain. • Mehndi chhudane ke liye paani ka istemal na kare. Ho sake toh 10 se 12 ghanto tak haatho par paani na padne de. Saabun ke istemal se door rahe toh hi behtar hoga. Mehndi chhudane ke baad sarso ke tel ko haatho par mal le. In upay ko apnane se mehndi ka rang gahra chadhta hain.
