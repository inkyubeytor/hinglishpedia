Aajkal ek phone par ek se jyada SIM chalana aam si baat ho gyi hain. Aise mein kuch numbers aapko acche messaging offers dete hain, toh kuch acche internet offers. Kuch sim aapko baat karne ke liye acchi connectivity dete hain, toh kuch der tak baat karne ke liye acche offers. Aise mein numbers ka bhoolna aapke liye ek aam si baat hain. Agar aapke paas koi dusra mobile nahi hain aur aap apna mobile number bhool gye ho toh ise kaise pata karenge? Aise milega aapko aapka mobile number  Ab agar aapke paas agar koi dusra mobile hain toh aap uska number dial karke, oos phone par apna mobile number jaan sakte hain. Lekin aapke paas agar dusra mobile nahi hain toh aapki help karenge kuch aise khas USSD codes jinko apne hi phone par dial karne se veh dikhayenge aapke hi mobile par aapka number. Behad chote hain yeh USSD codes Yeh codes behad chote hain, aap inhe badi asaani ke saath yaad rakh sakte hain. Ek sim ke liye kai USSD codes suggest kiye jayenge, Inme aapke circle area ke hisaab se kisi bhi ek number ko dial karne par yeh aapki screen par aapka number show kar dega. Example ke taur par Vodafone ke sim ko hi le lijiye. Agar aap is company ka sim istemal kar rahe hain toh neeche diye gye codes mein kisi ek code par aapko aapka number screen par dikh jayega. Kariye isko try. Vodafone ke liye codes :  *111*2# *555# *555*0# *777*0# *131*0# inme se aapke circle area ke hisaab se kisi bhi ek number ko dial karne par aap paa sakenge apna mobile number.
