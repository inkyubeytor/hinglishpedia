Agar aap apne carrier mein teacher banne ki chah rakhte hain toh iska sabse pahla step hain C.T.E.T. Exam. Is exam ko clear karke aap teacher banne ke race mein shamil ho sakte hain. Iske baad sambandhit schools ke pratiyogi exam pass karke aap teacher ban sakte hain. Janiye CTET exam ke bare mein :- Kab hota hain aayojit ?  CTET exam saal mein 2 baar aayojit kiya jata hain. CBSE CTET offline hi conduct karati hain. Kaun kar sakta hain CTET clear ? Central Teacher Eligibility Test mein 60% ya usse jyada marks laane wale candidates hi clear kar sakte hain. Kya hain exam ? Exam clear karne ka matlab naukri milna nahi hain. Iska matlab hain ki applicant Government school mein Teacher banne ke liye yogya (Eligible) ho gya hain. Result declare hone ke baad marks certificate ko 7 saalo tak valid mana jata hain. Yeh exam candidates chahe jitni baar de sakte hain. Ek baar qualify kar chuke candidates apna score better karne ke liye dobara bhi yeh exam de sakte hain. Kaha mil sakti hain naukri ? CTET se aap Kendriya Vidyalaya, Navodaya Vidyalaya, Sarvodaya Vidyalaya , Central Tibetan Schools aur sabhi Government Schools mein padhane ke yogya ho jate hain. Yahi nahi acche private schools mein bhi CTET ki demand hain.  Kaun de sakta hain yeh test ? Is exam mein vahi participate kar sakta hain, Jisne B.Ed kiya ho ya uske paas Diploma in alimentary education ho.  Kaisa hota hain exam ? CTET exam mein pahle paper mein 5 section hote hain. Har section mein 30 question hote hain. In questions mein Child development, Language I aur Language II, Maths aur environment ke subjects se jude swaal shamil hote hain.  Jyada Jaankari ke liye aap CTET ki Official Website par bhi login kar sakte hain.
