Black Tea (Kali Chai) kaise banaye? Black Tea banane ki vidhi (Recipe) :- 1 cup paani ko ubale, jab paani khaulne lage to aadha chammach chaipatti daale. Phir kuch der ke liye isme ubaal aane de. Agar jaroorat pade to aap isme 1 ya aadha chammach Chini bhi daal sakte hain. Phir uske baad chai jab ban kar taiyaar ho jaye to Nimbu ka ras mila kar piye. Cold Black Tea :- Cold Black tea aapka natural Cold Drink ban sakta hain. Iske liye 1 cup paani ko boil kare, usme 1 chammach Chini daale, phir aadha chammach Chai patti daale. Phir ubaal aane ke baad usay thanda kare. Phir usme Nimbu ka ras milaye aur phir usme Ice ya burf ke tukade daale. Lijiye taiyaar hain aapki natural cold drink. Yeh sehat ke liye bahut hi acchi hain aur ise peene se cold drink (soft drink) ki tarah koi nuksaan bhi nahi hota hain.
