Bina dawaai khaye kaise bukhaar ko kam kiya jata hain? Dawaai khaye bagair bukhaar (Fever) ko door ya kam kaise kare? Jab kisi bacche ko bukhaar ho jata hain, to jyadatar log turant First Aid box se koi fever ki medicine nikaal kar de dete hain. Aapko apne baccho ko in chemicals ko nigalne ke liye majboor karne se pahle, bukhaar kam karne ke kuch badhiya aur natural tariko ko apnane ki koshish karni chahiye. Bukhar hone par sharir mein paani ki matra, Garm aur saaf rahna na bhoole, to bukhaar ek ya do din mein utar jayega. Bina dawaai ke bukhaar theek karne ke liye apnaye yeh upay aur tarike :-  1. Seer aur Gardan ko thanda kare. Bahut tez bukhaar ke liye, Ek bada Sooti kapda paani mein acchi tarah se duboye. Phir ise nichod le. Kapde ko bacche ke seer aur Gardan ke chaaro aor lapete aur jab kapda sookh jaye, to ise phir se dohraye. 2. Imli ke ras ya Aloe Vera Gel ya Amrood ki patti ke ras ka istemal karke sharir ke temperature ko tezi ke saath kam kiya ja sakta hain. Ras ko maathe par lagaye, Ise sookhne de aur lagaye, Jab tak sharir ka tapmaan kam na ho jaye. Ghar mein hawa ke liye pankha chalaye. Late jaye aur theek se aaram kare. 3. Tel malish kare. 2 Saal ke kam umar ke baccho ke liye, sone se pahle unke sharir ko shuddh Jaitun ke tel se malish karke unhe acchi tarah se Sooti kapde aur ek kambal mein lapet de. Tel ko hatane ke liye unhe subah nahlaye. Yeh step geele moje ke saath acchi tarah se kaam karta hain. 4. Moje geele karke unhe aidiyo mein pahanna ajeeb lagta hain, Lekin baccho ke tez bukhar ko kam karne ke liye yeh badhiya kaam karta hain. Ek jodi sooti moje le jo bacche ki aidiyo ko cover karne ke liye sahi ho. Moje ko nal ke thande paani se theek se geela kar le. Extra paani ko nichod de. Mojo ko bacche ke pairo par rakh de aur jab moje sookh jaye to is process ko repeat kare. 5. Paani ka khoob sewan kare. Bukhaar ke virus  se ladne ke liye aapke sharir ki process hoti hain, Lekin adhik taap ka matlab paani ki kami hain. Paani ki kami na hone de, khaskar Dast ya Ulti hone par , Kyonki isse sharir mein paani ki kami ho jati hain. 6. Garam ya Gungune paani se nahaye. Gungune paani se naha kar daude. Bukhaar se grast vyakti ko koodne de aur usay chhod de. to Garam temperature dheere-dheere niche aata hain. Vyakti temperature par kam dhyaan deta hain. 7. Halke kapde pahne, Mote kapde na pahne. Kambal na lapete chahe thand hi kyon na mahsoos ho rahi ho, Kyonki isse aapke body ke temperature mein ijafa hoga.
