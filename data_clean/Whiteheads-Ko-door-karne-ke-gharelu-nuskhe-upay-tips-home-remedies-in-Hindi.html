Phir 20 minutes baad chehre ko saaf paani se dho le. • Tea Bag :- Chai banane ke baad istemal kiya hua Tea bag se aap white heads ko door kar sakte hai. White heads saaf ho jayenge. • Aloe Vera gel :- Chehre ko pani se dhone ke baad uspar Aloe Vera gel lagaye, Isse aapko labh hoga. Aisa rojana karne se white heads dheere-dheere khatm ho jate hain. • Apple Cider Vinegar :- Apple Cider Vinegar whiteheads ko sookha deta hain. Apne chehre ko garm paani se bhaap dene ke baad thodi si rooi ko apple cider vinegar mein bhigo kar infected areas par lagaye, Uske baad chehre ko kuch der tak na dhoye. • Paani :- Daily 3 litter paani piye aur chehra oily hain toh usay din mein kai baar paani se dhoye. • Nimbu :- Apne face par Nimbu ka ras halke halke haatho se ragde.
