Lava ne naya entry level smartphone, Flair P1 ko launch kiya hain. Iski keemat 3,399 Rupaye rakhi gyi hain. Yeh phone Black aur white colour mein pure desh bhar ke retail stores, Multi-Brand outlets aur online stores par available hain.  Company ke mutabik, aane wale kuch mahine mein is series ke tahat 3 aur smartphone launch hone ki umeemd hain.  Lava Flair P1 mein 4 inch WVGA (480 X 800 pixel) TFT display hain. Saath hi ismein 1 GHz processor, 256 MB RAM, 2 GB internal Memory hain jise micro SD card ke jariye 32 GB tak badhaya ja sakta hain. Yeh phone Android ke 4.4 KiKat Operating system par operate hota hain.  Iske saath hi phone mein LED flash ke saath 2 Megapixel rear camera aur VGA front Camera hain. Phone mein 1400 mAh ki battery hain. Yeh Phone 3G, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 2.0 aur A-GPS ko support karta hain.
