Kaddu (Sitaphal) ke beej khane ke fayde/labh kya hain.  Hum mein se jyadatar log sabziyo ke poshak chhilko aur beejo ko faltu samajh kar phenk dete hain. Example ke taur par jaise ki kaddu ki sabzi ban rahi ho toh aksar hum uske beejo ko dustbin mein daal dete hain. Lekin yeh beej hamare swasthya ke nazriye se bahut hi faydemand  mane  gye hain. Aaiye jante  hain kaddu ke beej se hone wale swasthyvaradhak labh (fayde) aur benefits ke baare mein aur bhi bilkul asaan Hindi/Urdu mein :-  • Dil aur Liver ke rogo mein faydemand :- Alsi aur Kaddu ke beejo ko barabar  matra mein (kareeb 2 gram) rojana ek baar lena chahiye. Aisa mana jata hain ki yeh Liver ki kamjori aur heart ki problems mein kafi kargar hote hain. Journal Food Chemistry & Toxicology mein publish 2008 ki ek report bhi iss tarah ke  daawo ko sahi thaharaati hain.  • Pathaari ya Kidney stone mein faydemand :- Saal 1987 mein American Journal mein publish ek research ke mutabik jin baccho ke peshab test sample mein Calcium Oxalate Particles  paaye gye, Unke khaane mein kaddu ke beejo ko shamil kiya gya aur iss problem ko kafi hud tak kam hote dekha gya. Calcium Oxalate  kidney mein stone  ko paida karte hain.  • Ghaavo ko theek kare :- Sharir ke jin hisso par ghaav ya kisi tarah ka infection ho gya ho. Oon jagaho par Kaddoo ke sookhe beezo ka churan ya taza beejo ka ras lagane se aaram milta hain.  • Periods se judi problems ke liye :- Saal 2008 mein aayi ek report ke mutabik Jin mahilaon ko kaddu ke beej ke tel (2ml.) ka sewan rojana 12 hafto tak karaya gya. Unme  periods ke  dauran  hone wali problems, Jaise blood pressure badhna, cholesterol ka badhna, Hormones ki kami ka hona aadi mein sudhaar  dekha gya. Dil ki bimariyo aur blood  circulation se judi dusri problems mein kaddu ke beejo se  nikaala gya tel  behad  ki kargar bataya jata hain.  • Prostate badhne se roke :- Prostate ko badhne se rokne ke liye kaddu ke beejo ko kafi kargar  mana gya hain. 2008 mein aayi ek report mein Kaddu ke beejo ke tel se prostate ki badhotari  ko kam paya gya. Mana jata hain ki prostate granthi  badhne se  preshan rogi  ko rojana 4-5 gram kaddu ke beejo ka sewan jaroor karna chahiye.  • Haath pair mein jalan ko door kare  :- Haath-pair mein jalan hone par kaddu ke beejo ko peeskar iska lep jalan wali jagaho par lagaye. turant rahat milegi. Yeh high blood pressure  ko kam karne mein behad  kargar saabit huye hain.
