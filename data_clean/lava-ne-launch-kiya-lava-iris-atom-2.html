Indian Handset maker company Lava ne kafi sasta Android Smartphone market mein launch kiya hain. Company ne is naye handset Iris Atom 2 ki keemat 4,949 rupaye rakhi hain. Aaiye jante hain hi is smartphone mein sabse khas baat kya hain aur kya yeh ek budget phone hain. 4.5 inch ki hain display  Lava Company ne apne is naye smartphone Iris Atom 2 mein Android ka Operating system diya hain. Isme aapko Android ka Kitkat Os milega. Vahi is handset mein aapko 4.5 inch ki display milegi. Iske alawa agar is handset ke processor par gaur kare to isme aapko 1.3GHz ka Quad-Core processor milega. Iske saath hi Iris Atom 2 mein aapko 512 MB ki RAM milegi. Vahi iski storage capacity ki baat kare to isme 8 GB ki internal memory aur 32 GB tak Micro SD card ki suvidha milegi. 5 Megapixel ka rear camera Lava Company ne apne is naye handset Iris Atom 2 ko kafi stylish banaya hain. Iska behtarin look users ko attract kar sakta hain. Isme aapko 5 Megapixel ka rear camera milega, Jisme LED flash hoga. Iske saath hi VGA front camera bhi isme mauzood hain. Agar connectivity ki baat kare to Iris Atom 2 mein aapko 3G, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi aur GPS aadi ki suvidha milegi. Halaki battery back-up mein yeh kuch khas nahi hain. Isme aapko 1750 mAh ki battery milegi. Color Variants ki baat kare to isme aapko Black aur White color hi milega. Full Specification of Lava Iris Atom 2 Smartphone :-  Model lava Iris Atom 2 Sim Dual SIM Display 4.5-inch (480 x 854 pixels) IPS display Memory RAM 512MB, ROM 8GB, External Card – 32GB Connectivity GPRS, EDGE,3G,Wifi, Bluetooth, USB Camera Front- 0.3, Rear – 5MP with Flash OS Android 4.4.2 Kitkat CPU 1.3 GHz quad-core processor GPU Adreno 306 Battery 1750 mAh Price Rs. 4,949
