Yeh breast , Skin , Pet aur colon jaisi jagaho par hone wale cancer ke bacteria se bachata hain.  2- Kidney ke rog se bachav  Roj ek glass Orange juice peene se kidney mein hone wale dikkato se chhutkara mil jata hain.  3- Liver Cancer ka risk kam hona  Japan ki ek study ke anusaar Orange ka juice peene se Liver mein hone wale cancer ka khatra kam ho jata hain. Aisa mana gya hain ki Orange Juice mein Vitamin A ki matra adhik hoti hain. Ise ham Carotenoids ke naam se jante hain.  4- Kam cholesterol Orange mein Fiber ki matra adhik hoti hain. Isliye yeh Cholesterol ko kam rakhne mein yeh madad karta hain.  5- Boost heart health  Orange mein potassium aur electrolyte minerals dil ke liye sahi rahte hain. Sharir mein Potassium ki matra kam hone par dil ki dhadkan kam hone lagti hain, jise arrhythmia kahte hain.  6- Bimariyon se bachav Orange mein Vitamin C bharpoor matra mein hota hain. Free Radicals cancer aur dil se judi dikkaton mein bachav karta hain.  7- Viral Infection Study ke anusaar, Orange mein polyphenols hota hain, jo Body ko viral infection se bachane mein madad karta hain. 8- Kabz door karne ke liye  Santre mein fiber hota hain , Vah bhojan ko pachane aur kabz door karne mein madad karta hain. 9- Aankhon ke liye  Orange mein carotenoids Compound aur Vitamin A hota hain jo aankhon mein padne wale dhabbon ko kam karta hain.   10- Blood pressure ko sahi rakhta hain. Orange mein magnesium aur  Hesperidin paya jata hain jo blood circulation ko sahi rakhta hain 11- Skin protection  Orange mein Beta-carotene hota hain, Jismein Anti-Oxidants paya jata hain.
