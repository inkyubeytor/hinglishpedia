Badalta mausam thanda-garam paani peena ya phir dhool ya kisi bhi tarah ki allergy ke karan Khansi ki samsya ho sakti hain. Khansi ki samsya hone par aap koi kaam skoon se nahi kar pate hain. Yadi aap bhi khansi ki samsya se preshan hain to apnaye yeh gharelu tarike :- 1. Raat ko sone se pahle ek glass garam doodh mein aadha chammach haldi mila kar piye. Isse Khansi mein bahut jaldi aaram milta hain. 2. Laung ko Ghee mein senk kar mooh mein rakhne se baar-baar Khansi nahi aati hain. 3. Subah Shaam Chane barabar matra mein bhuni huyi Fitkari ke powder ke saath khane se Khansi ki samsya door ho jati hain. 4. Lagbhag 2 cup paani mein Adrak ke chhote-chhote tukade aur kuch pattiya Imali ki dale. Ise tab tak ubale jab tak yeh ek cup na rah jaye. Isme 4 chammach Shakkar dal kar, Dheemi aanch par kuch der aur ubaale. Phir thanda hone de. Thanda hone par isme 10 boonde Nimbu ki dal de. Har teen ghante ke baad is syrup ka sewan karne se Khansi chhoo-mantar ho jati hain. 5. Shahad aur Kachche Pyaz ka ras saman matra mein lekar mila le. Ise 3-4 ghante ke liye kisi andhere wali jagah par rakh de. Baad mein ise piye, Yeh Khansi ki dawai ke taur par kaam karti hain. 6. Dhaniya, Jeera aur Bach barabar matra mein milaye aur kadha bana le. Sardi se preshan bacchon ko khane ke baad (10 ml) matra mein yeh kadha de. Khansi ki samsya door ho jayegi. 7. Khansi hone par Haldi ki chhoti gaanth mooh mein rakh kar choose. 8. Tulsi, Kali Mirch aur Adrak ki chaaye Khansi mein Ramban dawa ki tarah kaam karti hain. 9. Paani mein Gud, Adrak, Nimbu ka Ras, Ajwain, Haldi ko barabar matra mein dalkar ubaale aur phir ise chhankar piye. Khansi mein bahut jaldi aaraam milega. 10. Adrak, Laung, Heeng aur Namak ko mila kar pees le aur iski chhoti-chhoti goliya taiyar kare. Din mein 3-4 baar ek goli choose. 11. Mulethi ki Chaaye bana kar peene se bhi Khansi mein jaldi aaram milta hain.Â
