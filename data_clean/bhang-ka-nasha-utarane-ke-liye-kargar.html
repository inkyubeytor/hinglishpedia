Holi ke festival mein kai sare log bhaang pee lete hain. Agar kisi ko bhaang ka nasha chadh gya hain to aap neeche batayi gyi tips aur gharelu nuskhe ko ajma sakte hain. Isse bhang ka nasha ekdum se utar jata hain. Bhaang ke nashe ki wajah se aadmi behosh bhi ho sakta hain. Lekin uska nasha utaarne ke liye aapko bas asaan tariko ko ajmaane ki jaroorat hain.  Bhaang ka nasha utaarne ke tarike :-  • Ek glass Nimbu ka ras peene se bhi bhaang ka nasha kam ho jata hain.  • Ek ya Do glass Chaach (Lassi) peene se bhi bhaang ka nasha utara ja sakta hain.  • Bhaang ke nashe ki wajah se hui behoshi ko door karne ke liye Sarso ke tel ko garm kare. Gunguna hone par tel ki boonde patient ke dono kaano mein daale.  • Ek katori meetha dahi khilaye.  • Ek chota sa Adrak ka tukda khaye.  • Ek glass Nariyal Paani pijiye.  • 3 ya 4 chammach Ghee khaye.  • Ek katori makkhan khaye.  • Ek glass paani mein 6 ya 7 Imli ghol kar piye.  • Ayurvedic Doctor ki salah ke baad Brahmi Vati. Saraswatarishta ya Ashwagandharishta Jaisi Ayurvedic Medicine le sakte hain.
