Haldi hamari rasoi ka ahem hisaa hain. Iske bagair koi bhi sabzi nahi banayi ja sakti hain. Haldi ko English mein Turmeric kahte hain, Ise masalo ka raja bhi kaha jata hain. Yeh ek aisa masala hain jise kai cosmetic beauty products mein bhi istemal kiya jata hain. Aaj hum janenge ki Haldi ko kyun khana chahiye. Ise apne khane mein istemal karne se hame kya labh hote hain. Daadi maa ke gharelu nuskho mein Haldi ka upyog chot, moch, andoorni ghaav, jakhm, sardi-jukaam aur khansi jaisi bimariyo ko theek karne ke kiya jata raha hain. Haldi beauty product, dharmik kaamo aur mangalik karyo mein hamesha se istemal hote aayi hain.
