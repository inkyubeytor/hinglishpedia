Chai peene ki wajah se dhamniya chikni aur cholesterol se free ho jati hain. 6 Cup se jyada chai peene se dil ki bimari hone ka khatra 1/3 kam rahta hain. 2. Daant bane majboot ;- Chai peene se aapke daant majboot banenge. Jee haan, Agar aap bina chini wali chai piyenge toh aisa jaroor hoga. Chai asal mein Fluoride aur Tannin se bani hoti hain jo plague ko door rakhta hain. Toh healthy gums aur teeth ke liye tea peena shuru kar dijiye. 3. Fat kam kare :- Chaay peene se aapke body ka metabolism badh jata hain. Kai logo ki shikayat hoti hain ki exercise karne ke baad bhi unka wajan kam nahi ho raha hain, Lekin agar aap green tea piyenge toh aapka metabolism rate badhega jisse 70 se 80 Calorie aaram se burn ho sakti hain. Iske saath hi aapko rojana 30 minutes ki walk lena bhi jaroori hain. 4. Rog se lade :- Chaay peene se aapka sharir ki prati-raksha pranali ko infection se ladne mein bahut madad milti hain. Sardi Jukhaam jaisi aam bimariya chaay peene se ekdum gayab ho jati hain. 5. Umar ghataye :- Chaay mein Anti-oxidants shamil hote hain. Chai umar ke badhne aur pollution ke side-effects se aapko bachata hain. 6. Haddiya bane majboot :- Chai aapki haddiyo ko bhi bachaati hain. Kewal isliye nahi ki isme doodh mila hua hota hain, Balki ek research mein oon logo ka compare ek saath kiya gya jo Chai ka upyog 10 saalo se karte aa rahe hain aur jo chai nahi peete. Paani ki kami puri kare ;- Chai dehydrate rahne mein help karti hain. Isliye hamare sharir mein water ki supply nahi ho paati hain. Agar aap din bhar mein 5 se 6 cup coffee ke pee jate hain toh aapko paani ki kami ho sakti hain. 8. Kam calorie :- Chai Calorie free hoti hain. Chai mein kisi bhi tarah ki calorie nahi hogi, Jab tak aap usme kisi bhi tarah ka sweetener ya doodh na mila le. Agar aap santoshjanak , calorie free drink peena chahte hain toh chai usme se ek  hain. 9. Kam Caffeine :- Chai mein coffee ke mukable kam caffeine hoti hain. Agar aapko coffee peene se apach, seerdard ya sone mein koi problem ho to bina soche chaai ki aor rookh kare. 10. Cancer se bachaye :- Chai cancer se hame bachaati hain, kyonki isme Polyphenols aur  Anti-Oxidants mila hota hain. Jo cancer se ladne mein bahut madad karte hain.
