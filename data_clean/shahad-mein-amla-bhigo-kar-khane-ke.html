Iska swaad chahe kitna bhi kadwa aur kasaila kyon na ho, Lekin yeh Vitamin C ka powerhouse hain.  Saath hi isme kai jaroori nutrients paye jate hain. Amla khane se sharir ki kai bimariyo ke saath-saath yeh aapko jawan banaye rakhta hain. Isliye aapko rojana Amla khane ki aadat dalni chahiye. Amla mein ek khas baat yeh hoti hain ki yeh sookhne par bhi iska vitamin C khatm nahi hota hain. Lekin kya jante hain ki Amla ko shahad mein bhigo kar khane se aapko aur bhi zyada healthy benefits aur fayde hote hain.  Amla ko shahad mein bhigo kar kyun khana chahiye ?  Shahad mein Amla ko bhigone se na kewal Amla kai mahino tak theek rahta hain. Ise banane ke liye ek jar ko aadha shahad se bhar le aur phir usme Amla bhigo kar dhakkan ko band karde.
