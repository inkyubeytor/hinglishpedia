Indian Handset maker company celkon ne apna naya handset Campus Q405 smartphone ko market mein utaar diya hain. Company yeh budget smartphone e-commerce website par available hain, Jiski keemat 3,199 Rupaye rakhi gyi hain. 4 inch ki hain display Celkon company ne apne is naye smartphone Campus Q 405 mein Android Operating system diya hain. Isme aapko 4.4.2 KitKat OS milega. Company ne isme 4 Inch ki display lagaai hain. Iske alawa agar handset ke processor par gaur kare to isme aapko 1.2 GHz ka Quad-Core processor milega. Campus Q405 mein aapko 512 MB ki RAM milegi. Isme aapko 4 GB ki internal memory aur 32 GB tak micro SD card ki suvidha milegi. 3.2 Megapixel ka hain rear camera  Celkon Company ne apne is naye handset Campus Q405 mein 3.2 Megapixel ka rear camera diya hain, Jisme auto-focus hoga. Vahi isme aapko 1.3 Megapixel ka front camera bhi milega. Agar connectivity ki baat ki jaye to Campus Q405 mein aapko 3G, GPRS/ EDGE, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi aadi ki suvidha milegi. Halaki battery backup isme kuch khas nahi hain. Isme aapko 1500 mAh ki battery milegi. Philhaal colour variant ki baat kare to ismein aapko White, Gold, Blue aur Black colour milega.
