Indian Handset Maker company Videocon ne apna naya smartphone Z55 dash launch kar diya hain. Is smartphone ki khas baat yeh hain ki ise puri tarah se India mein hi design kiya gya hain. Halaki, keemat ki baat kare to yeh E-commerce website par 6,499 rupaye mein mil raha hain. 5 inch ki hain display  Videocon Company ne apne is naye smartphone Z55 dash mein Android ka operating system diya hain. Isme aapko Android ka 4.4.2 Kitkat Os milega. Vahi handset mein aapko 5 inch ki display milegi. Iske alawa agar is handset ke processor par gaur kare to isme aapko 1.4 GHz ka Octa-Core MediaTek6735 Processor milega. Vahi Z55 Dash mein aapko 1 GB ki RAM milegi. Agar iski storage capacity ki baat ki jaye to isme aapko 8 GB ki internal Memory milegi, Jise 32 GB tak Micro-SD card ki madad se expand kiya ja sakta hain. 8 MP ka rear Camera  Videocon company ne apne is naye handset Z55 Dash ko kafi stylish banaya hain. Iska behatarin look users ko attract kar sakta hain. Isme aapko 8 megapixel ka rear camera milega, jisme LED flash hoga. Iske saath hi isme aapko 5 megapixel ka front camera bhi milega. Agar connectivity ki baat ki jaye to Z55 dash mein aapko 3G, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi aur GPS aadi ki suvidha milegi. Battery Backup mein bhi yeh smartphone kafi jabardast hain. Isme aapko 2200 mAh ki battery milegi. Color variant ki baat kare to isme aapko Black & white aur White Chrome color milega.
