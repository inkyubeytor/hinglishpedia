Tamatar ke bare mein kya kahe, Yeh bahut hi tasty aur ras se bhare huye phal hain. Ji haan tamatar asal mein sabzi nahi balki phal hain. Lekin roj marra ki jaroorato ke chalte hum ise sabzi ke roop mein istemal karte aa rahe hain. Tamatar mein Vitamin A,C, K, Folate aur Pottasium bahut hi acchi matra mein paye jate hain. Tamatar mein Fat, Cholesterol , Sodium aur Calorie kam matra mein hote hain. Tamatar Thiamine, Niacin, Vitamin B-6, Magnesium, Phosphorus aur Copper ka bahut accha source hain. Itna sab kuch janne ke baad aap jaroor janna chahenge ki aakhir Tamatar khane se sehat ko kya-kya labh hote hain. Tamatar (Tomato) khane se health ko yeh fayde hote hain. • Cancer se ladta hain :- Tamatar natural tarike se cancer se ladta hain. Prostate, Grabhashya, Mooh, Gala, Pet, Colon aur bhi kai tarah ke cancer ke khatre ko aap sirf tamatar kha kar kam kar sakte hain. Tamatar mein mauzood Anti-Oxidants , Free Radicals se ladte hain, jo hamare body cells ko damage karne ka kaam karta hain. • Aankho ke liye accha :- Tomato mein Vitamin A bahut hi acchi matra mein paya jata hain, Jo ratondhi ko rokne mein madad karta hain aur aapke aankho ki roshini ko badhata hain. Ek research se pata chala hain ki Tamatar khane se aankho ki dhabbedaar andh-vikriti ko kam karne mein madad milti hain. • Dil ke liye accha hain :- Tamatar mein paya jane wala potassium aur Vitamin B cholesterol level ko kam karte hain aur iske saath hi tamatar heart attack aur stroke ke khatre ko bhi kam kar deta hain. Healthy heart ke liye tomato ko apne bhojan mein jaroor shamil kare. • Wajan kam karne mein faydemand :- Tamatar khane se aap apna wajan kam kar sakte hain. Kyonki Tamatar mein dher saara paani aur Fiber hota hain, Iske weight loss karne ki soch rahe hain toh tamatar ko apne khane mein salad, soup aadi ke roop mein shamil kare, Weight loss karne ke liye tamatar ko feeling food kaha jata hain, Kyonki yeh pet ko jaldi bhar deta hain aur veh bhi bina calorie aur fat ko badhaye. • Healthy Skin ke liye :- Tamatar aapki skin ko bahut hi badhiya bana deta hain. Gajar aur Chukander mein paya jane wala Beta-Carotene tamatar mein bhi paya jata hain, Jo Suraj ki ultra-violet rays se tavcha ki raksha karta hain. Tamatar mein paya jane wala Lycopene Ultra-Violet rays ko kam sensitive banata hain, Jo skin par lines aur wrinkles hone ki ahem wajah mani gyi hain. • Kidney Stone aur Pitt ki patthari ko rokta hain : - Tamatar gurde aur pitt ki patthari ko rokne mein madad karta hain. Lekin iske liye aapko bina beej wale tamatar khane padenge. • Haddiya banaye majboot :- Tamatar mein Vitamin K aur Calcium dono hi haddiyo ko majboot banane ke saath hi unki murramat bhi karte hain. Lycopene Haddiyo ko sudhaarta hain, Jo osteoporosis se ladne mein madad karta hain. • Dard Door kare :- Agar aap bhi purane dard se preshan hain toh tamatar ise khatm kar sakta hain. Tamatar mein high Bioflavonoids aur Carotene naam ka tatv hota hain, Jo pain ko door karne mein help karta hain. • Balo ko healthy banaye :- Tamatar mein paya jane wale vitamins baalo ko majboot banate hain. Lekin yeh patle hote ja rahe baalo mein fayda nahi pahuchata hain but ise khane se aapke baal healthy rahenge. • Madhumeh mein faydemand :- Tamatar aapke blood sugar level ko control mein rakhta hain. Tomato mein chromium naam ka tatv paya jata hain jo blood sugar ko control karne mein madad karta hain. • Kaccha Tamatar khana hota hain faydemand :- Tamatar mein Lycopene, Vitamin A aur C hote hain. Yeh Anti-Oxidants ka khazana hain. Vitamin C jo kacche tamatar se milta hain veh aag par pakaaye gye tamatar se nahi milta hain. Tamatar ke behtarin fayde paane ke liye aapko tamatar ko kaccha aur pakaye gaye tamatar ko mix karke khana chahiye.
