Aaj ke samay mein Baal jhadne aur girne ki problems se har  koi preshan rahta hain. Agar samay rahte aapke baalo ko girne se bachane ke liye koi ilaj nahi khoja toh yeh dheere-dheere kam hote chale jayenge aur aap ganje ya ganji ho jayenge. Baal jhadne ki problem ke ilaj ke liye aap Nariyal tel (coconut Oil) ka istemal kar sakte hain. Aaj hum aapko kuch asaani upay aur nuskhe batane ja rahe hain, jinhe istemal karke aap ghane, lambe aur khubsoorat baal pa sakti hain. Yeh sabhi nuskhe nariyal tel ke istemal se kiye ja sakte hain.  • Nariyal tel, Dahi aur Nimbu ka istemal :- Ek katori mein ek anda fodiye aur usme 2 chammach nariyal tel aur Dahi milaye. Ab is mishran ko apne baalo mein 20 minutes tak laga rahne de aur phir uske baaad baalo ko paani se dho le. Isse baal mulayam aur shiny ho jayenge.  •  Nariyal Tel aur Anda :- Ande mein 1 chammach coconut oil mix kare aur inhe apne baalo par lagaye. ise 20-25 minutes tak baalo par lagaye rakhe aur phir shampoo se baal dho le. Isse baal shilky aur shiny ho jayenge.  • Coconut oil & honey :- Dono mein hi moisuturizer hota hain jisse baalo mein shine aata hain. 2 Chammach nariyal ke tel mein 1 chammach honey mix kare. Isse apne balo ki massage kare. 10-15 minutes baad ise shampoo se dho le.  • Nariyal tel aur Nimbu :- Thode se nariyal tel mein kuch boonde nimbu ke ras ki milaye. Isse halke haatho se seer ki maalish kare aur baad mein shampoo se dho le. Isse baalo mein nami barkarar rahegi aur nimbu se dandruff se chhutkara milega.  •  Nariyal tel aur Heena :- Mehndi se baalo ka jhadna rook jata hain, lekin yeh baalo ko dry  bhi kar deti hain. Agar aap chahti hain ki baal rookhe na bane toh mehndi mein nariyal tel daal kar lagaye. Isse baalo mein nami barkarara rahegi.  • Nariyal tel aur Reetha :- Thode se nariyal tel mein 4 chammach reetha powder milaye. Ise 20 minutes tak baalo par lagaye aur phit  shampoo kar le, isse baalo ka jhadna band ho jata hain aur do-moohe baalo ki problem bhi door hoti hain.
