Jai ke saath doodh mila kar aap ek pack bana sakte hain jo asaani se aur asardar tarike se gardan aur kohni ke kaale dhabbe ko door kar sakta hain.  • Nimbu ka ras :- Nimbu ke ras mein Citrus Acid hota hain, Jiski wajah se hum usay natural bleaching agent kahte hain. Nimbu Vitamin C ka sabse accha source hain. Hum jante hain ki skin mein nikhar laane ke liye Vitamin C aur E sabse acche hote hain. Gardan aur Kuhani ke kaale hisse par nimbu ka ras lagane se kafi tak veh gora hone lagta hain.  • Papita aur Strawberry :- Papita aur Strawberry gardan ki skin ke upchar ke liye acche fruits saabit ho sakte hain. Strawberry mein dead cells ko hatane ke saare gun hote hain aur papite mein Antioxidant enzyme hote hain, jo scrub ke roop mein accha asar dikha sakte hain. Neck aur elbow par pade kisi bhi nishaan aur kaale dhabbe ko halka karne ke liye yeh bahut hi accha upay hain.  • Santre ka chhilka :- Santra Vitamin C se bharpoor  hota hain. Santre ke chhilke ko gardan aur kuhani se kaale daag mitane aur unme nikhaar laane ke liye istemal kar sakte hain.  • Kela :- Kela health ke liye bahut hi faydemand hota hain. Iske niyamit istemal se kaali gardan aur kaali kohni ko gora kiya ja sakta hain.  • Besan :- Besan ka istemal aap natural scrub ke roop mein kar sakte hain. Besan sust aur bezan padi skin mein jaan daal kar usko freshness ka ahsaas dilata hain aur dead skin ko hatane ka kaam natural tarike se karta hain. Kaali kuhani aur kali gardan ko besan ragadne se skin mein glow aane lagta hain.  • Aaloo ka ras :- Aaloo natural roop se skin ko nikhaarne aur usme safedi laane ke liye jana jata hain.
