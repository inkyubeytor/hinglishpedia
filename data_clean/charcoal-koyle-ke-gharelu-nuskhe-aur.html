Charcoal (Koyala/koyle) se bhi fayde hote hain. Bhale hi aapko yeh padhkar thoda ajeeb lage, lekin sach toh yeh hain ki charcoal se bhi sehat ko kai fayde hote hain. Khastaur par videsho mein Activated charcoal ka istemal kiya jata hain. Aayiye jante hain Charcoal se hone wale faydo ke bare mein :- 1) Paani mein mauzood gandagi ko door karne ke liye aur behtar safai ke liye charcoal bahut hi jyada upyogi hain. Isliye kai desho mein paani ki safai ke liye Charcoal ka istemal kiya jata hain. Safai ke gun ki wajah se iska istemal ab beauty products mein bhi kiya jane laga hain. 2) Industry se nikalne wale chemical ki zehareeli gandh se lekar gym mein pahne jane wale kapde ki badboo tak, Charcoal ka istemal air freshener tak kiya jata hain. Yeh gandgi ko sokhta hain aur saaf hawa lene aur fefdo ko surakshit rakhne ke liye faydemand hain. 3) Saanp kaatne ya kisi zehreele jeev-jantoo ka zehar utaarne ke liye Charcoal bahut hi kaam ki cheez mani jati hain. Kyonki isme Zehar virodhi tatv mauzood hote hain. Sahi waqt par iska istemal karne se zehar ko chadhne se roka ja sakta hain. Lekin sirf aapko charcoal ke bharoshe hi nahi rahna chahiye, balki aisi situation mein sahi ilaj bhi karwana chahiye. 4) Pet dard aur Aant se related problems ke liye kai jagaho par koyale ka istemal kiya jata hain. American Citizen dwara Charcoal ka powder aur paani ko mila kar pet ki gadbadiyo ko door karne ke liye istemal kiya jata hain. Aaj kal Activated Charcoal ka istemal Diarrhoea, Kabz aur pet marod jaisi problems ke ilaj ke liye kiya jata hain. Lekin Research iske effect ke bare mein mila-jula reaction dete hain. Isliye inhe ajmane se pahle doctor se jaroor contact kare. 5) Khane peene ki pack cheezo, Snacks, Candy aadi mein bhi ab Charcoal ka istemal kiya jane laga hain. Is tarah ki cheeze swaad mein toh acchi lagti hi hain aur sehat ke liye bhi faydemand hoti hain. 6) Khoon mein cholesterol ko sokhne aur usey kam karne ka gun Activated Chorcoal mein mauzood hain. Lekin Cholesterol se related problems ke liye doctor ki salah leni chahiye, tabhi kisi tarah ki dawa ka istemal kare.
