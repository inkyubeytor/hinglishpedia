Soup peena sehat ke liye bahut hi faydemand hota hain. Soup peene ke bahut hi fayde hain, bhojan se kuch samay pahle soup peene se bhookh khulti hain aur jyada poshan milta hain. Aam taur par log bimar hone par soup ka jyada sewan karte hain, lekin soup ko niyamit roop mein apni diet mein shamil karne se kai labh hote hain. Aaiye jante hain soup ke faydemand gun ke bare mein :- • Wajan kam kare :- Agar aap kam calorie lena pasand karte hain aur jaldi wajan kam karna chahte hain, toh soup se behtar kya ho sakta hain. Isme aapko fibers aur poshak tatv ki bharpoor matra milti hain aur calorie bhi jyada nahi hoti hain. Soup peene se pet jaldi bhar jata hain aur bharipan bhi nahi hota hain.   • Pachne mein asaan :- Soup ka sewan bimariyo mein isliye bhi kiya jata hain, kyonki yeh asaani se pach jata hain aur kisi tarah ki preshani paida nahi karta hain. Isse bimari ke baad sust pada pachan tantra bhi sahi tarike se kaam karne lagta hain.  • Mucus ko patla kare :- Kamjori hone par mucus mota ho jata hain, jiske karan bacteria aur virus ka khatra badh jata hain. Soup ka niyamit upyog karne se mucus patla ho jata hain, jisse infection nahi hota hain.   • Kamjori kare door :- Sharir mein kamjori mahsoos hone par soup ka sewan karna bahut hi faydemand hota hain. Yeh kamjori toh door karta hi hain, saath hi body ki immunity ko bhi badhata hain. Yeh bukhaar, sharirik dard, sardi-jukaam jaisi preshaniyo se ladne mein madad karta hain. Iske alawa tabiyat kharab hone par soup ka sewan se kisi bhi tarah ki preshani bhi nahi hoti hain.  • Taste badhaaye :- Agar aapke mooh ka swaad badal raha hain aur aapko har cheez beswaad lagne lagi hain toh phir soup pijiye. Isse aapko apna swaad vaapas lane mein madad milegi, kyonki yeh swaad badhaane mein madad karta hain.   • Paushtik :- Soup koi bhi ho , paushtik tatvo se bharpoor hota hain. Darasal jis sabzi ya khadya padarth ka soup banta hain, uska pura satv soup mein hota hain. Iske alawa kai tatvo se bharpoor soup androoni takat dene wala hota hain.   • Hydration :- Jab aap bimar hote hain ya fever se sharir dehydrate ho jati hain. Isliye aise waqt mein body ko hydrate rakhne ke liye soup pijiye. Isse sharir mein paani ki matra aur poshak tatv daakhil ho jate hain.   • Energy ke liye ;- Sharirik kamjori mein soup ka sewan aapko energy deta hain aur aap pahle se healthy feel karte hain. Dheere-dheere aapki energy level mein badhotari hone lagti hain aur aap healthy bante hain.  • Sardi-jukaam :- Sardi aur thand se bachne ke liye garma-garam soup behtar upay hain. Iske alawa jukaam hone ya gala kharaab hone ki situation mein Kali mirch mila hua soup peene se bahut jaldi aaram milta hain.   • Bhookh badhna :- Agar aapko bhookh nahi lagti ya kam lagti hain, toh soup peena accha option hain. Kyonki ise lene ke baad dheere-dheere bhookh khulne lagti hain aur bhojan ke taraf aapki ruchi badhti hain.
