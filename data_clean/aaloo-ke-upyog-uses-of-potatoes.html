Nimbu mein Vitamin C hota hain, Jo skin ke colour ko saaf karta hain. 2-  Wrinkle hone par  Chehre ki sikudan aur lines hone par Aaloo ka ras laga sakte hain. Saath hi skin Saaf aur soft bhi bani rahti hain. 3- Dark spot hone par  Chehre ya aankho ke niche dark spot hone par Aaloo sabse jyada faydemand hain. Massage karne ke baad thande paani se dho le. 4- Facial ke daag hone par  Kai baar aisa hota hain ki Koi facial hamare chehre ko suit nahi karta hain. Aise mein chehre par daag padne lagte hain. Aise mein Aaloo best remedy hain. Iske liye Aaloo ko kaat kar chehre par lagaye. 5- Sunburn hone par  Garmiyo mein Sunburn hone par Aaloo se behtar koi upay nahi hain. Iske liye aap Aaloo ki slice kaat kar fridge  mein rakh de. Uske baad thande Aaloo ko sunburn area par lagaye. Isse skin saaf, Smooth aur soft hogi. 6- Dark Circle ko remove karne ke liye  Aankhon ke dark circle ko door karne ke liye Aaloo ek natural upay hain.
