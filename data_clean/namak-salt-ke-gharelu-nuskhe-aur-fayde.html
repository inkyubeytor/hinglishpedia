Is nuskhe ko ajmaane se aapko kabz ki problems se aaram milega. • Muscles pain mein faydemand  Gungune paani mein 4-5 chammach Namak mila le. Isme cotton ka kapda bhigokar dard wali jagah par dheere-dheere se senkaai kare. Isse muscles pain mein rahat milegi aur dard door hoga. • Daanto ko safed banane ke liye  Ek Chammach Namak, ek chammach soda aur ek chammach Nimbu ka ras mix kare. Isse regular daanto par halke haathon se maalish kare. Isse daant majboot, chamkdaar aur bacteria free ban jate hain. • Digestion ke liye faydemand  Ek glass lassi mein thoda sa namak mila kar peene se digestion behtar hota hain. • Dandruff door kare  Aadha katori Khatte dahi mein ek chammach namak milaye. Phir is mixture ko apne baalo mein lagaye. Kareeb 30 minutes baad apne baalon ko dho le. Isse aapko dandruss se chhutkara milta hain. • Healthy skin ke liye  5 Chammach Namak ko 1 chammach Olive oil ke saath mix kare aur phir ise halke haatho se skin par massage kare. 10 minutes ke baad naha le. Isse skin soft aur healthy ban jati hain. • Soojan kam kare  Pairo mein sujan hone par gungune paani mein namak mila kar 10 minutes ke liye pairo ko isme duboye. Phir pairo ko towel se saaf karle, Isse pairo ki soojan kam karne mein madad milegi. • Chamakdaar naakhoono ke liye  Namak ko baking soda aur Nimbu ke ras mein mila le. Regular is paani se nahane par jodo ke dard se aaram milega. • Aankhon ke dard ke liye  Gulab jal mein chutaki bhar namak milaye. Ise 2 cotton balls mein bhigo kar 5 minutes ke liye aankhon par rakhe. Aankho ka dard aur Sujan door hogi.
