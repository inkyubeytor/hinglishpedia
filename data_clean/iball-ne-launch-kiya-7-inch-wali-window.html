IBall ne Indian users ke liye 7 inch wali Windows Tablet iBall Slide i701 lauch kar diya hain. Company ne is device ko 4,999 rupaye mein lauch kiya hain.  Launch ho gyi sabse sasti Tablet Agar aap ek sasti Window Tab khareedne ki taiyari kar rahe hain to bas aapka intezar khatm ho gya hain. Kyonki iBall ne apne Indian smartphone users ke liye 7 inch display wali Windows Tab launch kiya hain. Yeh Tab Windows ke 8.1 operating system par kaam karta hain. Company ka yeh daawa hain ki yeh duniya ki sabse sasta tablet hain. Iske alawa company ne yeh kaha hain ki yeh tablet Windows 10 ke liye upgrade hogi , jo ki is tablet ko khareedne walo ke liye ek achchi khabar hain. Milega Office Subscription  Is device par Microsoft Office ka subscription 1 saal ke liye diya gya hain. Iske saath hi Is tablet ke users ko Microsoft one drive par 1 saal tak 1TB data save kar sakenge. Agar features ki baat kare to Quad-core Intel Atom processor laga hain. Iske saath hi 1 GB ka DDR 3 RAM lagi huyi hain. Internal Memory 16 GB hain jise memory card ki madad se 32 GB tak badhaya ja sakta hain. Rear Camera 2 Megapixel ka hain to Front camera 0.3 Mega pixel ka hain.
