Chehre aur haath-pairo ki jitni hi jaroori hain gale ki khubsurati. Beautiful Necklace pahanane ke liye gale ka sunder hona bhi jaroori hain. Jyadatar log parlor jakar threading, bleaching, facial, Manicure, Pedicure aur waxing par hi focus karte hain. Veh bhool jate hain ki gale ka bhi khas khyaal rakhna jaroori hain. Iski andekhi karne par neck face se jyada kala aur ugly dikhai deta hain, saath hi lines bhi dikhti hain. Iski khubsurati ko gharelu upay aur nuskho ke jariye kaise barkarar rakha ja sakta hain, Aaiye jante hain.     ► Nariyal ka tel :- Vaise toh sabhi tarah ke tel skin ko komalta pradan karte hain, Lekin nariyal tel isme sabse jyada kargar mana jata hain. Istemal :- Nariyal ke tel mein Badam aur Olive Oil ki kuch boonde mila kar ise gale par lagaye. Raat bhar laga rahne de aur subah thande paani se dho le. Rojana istemal se kuch hi dino mein fark nazar aane lagega. ► Kheera :- Salad mein istemal ke saath hi Kheera moisturizing ke liye bhi istemal kiya jata hain. Yeh damaged cells ko repair karne ke saath hi dead skin ko bhi door karta hain aur rangat nikhaarta hain. Istemal :- Kheere ke tukde gale par lagaye aur sukhne de. 10 Minutes ke baad saaf paani se dho le. Gulabjal ka bhi istemal Kheere ke saath kiya ja sakta hain. ► Santre ka Chhilka :- Santre mein Vitamin C ki bharpoor matra payi jati hain. Iske chhilke ka istemal Bleach ke taur par kiya jata hain. Yeh skin ki darkness ko door karta hain. Istemal :- Santre ke chhilke ko doodh ke saath mila kar paste taiyaar kare. Nahane se pahle gale ke aas-paas lagakar 10 minutes tak massage kare aur fark dekhe. ► Chawal ka Paani :- Chawal ke paani mein mauzood starch komalta ke saath hi khoobsurti ke liye istemal kiya jata hain. Istemal :- 1 Cup chawal ko 2 cup paani ke saath ubaale. Chawal pakne ke baad gas ko band kar iska paani alag kar le. Thanda hone ke baad ise gale ke aas-paas ke hisse par lagakar 15-20 minutes ke liye chhod de. Sukhne par dho le. ► Besan :- Besan ka istemal dead skin ko nikaalne ke liye kiya jata hain. Saath hi isse komal aur mulayam tavcha bhi payi ja sakti hain. Istemal :- Besan mein Haldi aur Gulabjal ya Paani mila kar gale par lagaye. Todha sookhne par halka paani chhidkar massage karle aur phir dho le. Fark turant nazar aane lagega. ► Nimbu ka ras :- Vitamin C ka khazana Nimbu mein citric acid paya jata hain. Ise natural bleach ke taur par bhi istemal kiya jata hain. Khane mein Vitamin C aur E ki matra skin ke liye bahut hi faydemand hoti hain. Istemal :- Nahane se pahle Nimbu ka ras ko gardan par laga kar 5-10 minutes ke liye chhod de. Sukhne par naha le. Sensitive Skin ke liye Nimbu ke ras mein gulabjal mila kar istemal kiya ja sakta hain. ► Baking Soda :- Tavcha ki khoi hui rangat paane ke liye Baking soda bahut hi sasta aur accha gharelu upay hain. Istemal :- 1 Chammach Baking Soda ko paani ke saath mix kar paste banaye aur gardan par halke haatho se massage kare. 5 minute laga rahne de. Sookhne par paani se dho le. Hafte mein sirf 2 baar istemal gale ko komal aur chamakdaar banata hain. ► Aaloo ka ras :- Aaloo mein ek khas tarah ka enzyme catecholase  paya jata hain. Yeh tavcha ki rangat nikhaarne mein madadgar hota hain. Rojana iske istemal se bahut fayda hota hain. Istemal :- Nahane se pahle  sirf 10 minutes tak gale ke aas-paas kacche Aaloo ko gheese, Jisse iska juice gale ke chaaro tarah lag jaye. Sookhne par paani se dho le. Nimbu ka ras bhi milaya ja sakta hain.
