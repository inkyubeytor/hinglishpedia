Lava ne Bharatiya bazaar mein Iris X1 Atom S smartphone launch kar diya hain. Iski keemat mahaz 4,149 rupaye rakhi gyi hain. Yeh phone company ki website par available hain aur philhal iske 2 variants Black aur White mauzood hain.  Lava ka yeh Dual Sim Smartphone Android ke 4.4 Kitkat Operating system par chalta hain aur 3G connectivity ko support karta hain.  Is phone ko 4 inch ka TFT display diya gya hain aur yeh 1 GHz ke processor par chalta hain. Saath hi phone mein 512 MB ki RAM di gyi hain.  Phone ki internal Storage 8 GB ki hain, Jise micro SD card ki madad se 32 GB tak badhaya ja sakta hain. Itna hi nahi, Phone mein Wi-Fi Hotspot aur Micro USB jaise features bhi mauzood hain.  Phone ka price bhale hi behad kam hain, Iske bawjood LED flash ke saath 5 Megapixel ka rear camera diya gya hain. Phone mein front camera bhi hain.  Phone mein 1500 mAh ki battery di gyi hain, Jo ki 3G par 5 hours 41 minutes aur 2G par 14 hours ka talk time deti hain. Phone mein tamam censors aur preloaded apps bhi diye gye hain. Itna hi nahi phone ka look bhi kafi Sleek hain.
