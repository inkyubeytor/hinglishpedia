Indian Handset maker company Videocon ne Android based naya handset launch kiya hain. Yeh ek budget smartphone hain. Company ne is Z51 Nova smartphone ki keemat 5400 rupaye rakhi hain. 5 inch ki hain display  Videocon Company ne apne is naye Smartphone Z51 Nova mein Android Operating System diya hain. Isme aapko Android ka 4.4.2 KitKat Os milega. Vahi handset mein aapko 5 inch ki display milegi. Iske alawa agar handset ke processor par gaur kare to Isme aapko 1.2 GHz ka Quad-core processor milega. Saath hi 1GB ki RAM milegi. Vahi iske storage capacity ki baat kare to isme aapko 8GB ki internal memory milegi. 32GB tak Micro SD card ki suvidha milegi. 5 Megapixel ka rear camera  Company ne apne is naye smartphone Z51 Nova ko kafi stylish banaya hain. Iska behatrin look users ko attract kar sakta hain. Vahi isme 5 Megapixel ka Rear camera diya gya hain, Jisme LED flash hain. Jabki front camera 2 Megapixel ka hain. Agar Connectivity ki baat ki jaye to Z51 Nova mein aapko 3G, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi aur GPS aadi ki suvidha milegi. Iske alawa Battery Back-up mein yeh behtarin hain, Isme aapko 2000 mAh ki battery milegi. Colours variants ki baat kare to, Iske aapko Grey aur Blue colour milega. Full Specification :-  Model Videocon Z51 Nova Sim Dual SIM Display 5-inch (854 x 480 pixels) IPS display Memory RAM 1GB, ROM 8GB, External Card upto 32GB Connectivity GPRS, EDGE, 3G, Wifi, Bluetooth, USB Camera Front- 2, Rear – 5MP with Flash OS Android 4.4 (KitKat) CPU 1.2 GHz quad-core processor GPU Adreno 405 Battery 2000 mAh Price Rs. 5,400
