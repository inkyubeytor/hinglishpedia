In dino raat ko der se sona mano ek fashion sa ban gya hain. Aap raat se subah tak party karte hain aur phir halki si neend le kar dusre din office pahucha jate hain. Aaj kal yehi lifestyle pure desh ke youth mein phaili huyi hain. Kabhi kabhi office ka bhaari kaam padne se bhi neend puri nahi ho pati hain. Raat mein 6 ghante ki neend lena  hamare sharir ke liye bahut hi jaroori hain. Vaise toh aapko 8 ghante ki neend leni chahiye. Agar aap raat ko der se soyenge  aur din mein jaldi uthenge to iska nuksaan aapke dimaag ko bhugatna padta hain. Isse aapke sharir mein kai saari bimariya bhi paida ho sakti hain. Toh chaliye jante hain raat ko der se sone se hamare  sharir ko kya kya nuksaan hote hain.
