Lekin purane jamane mein jab Water Purifier nahi hote thay, Tab log Tambe ke bartan mein water ko store karke rakhte thay. Ise bahut hi accha purifier mana jata tha. Yeh fungal, algie aur paani mein mauzood kai kism ke bacteria aur germs ko easily destroy kar deta hain. Copper se body ko aur  kis-kis tarah se benefits milta hain aaiye jante hain. Yeh dhyaan rakhne wali baat hain ki Tambe ke bartan mein kam se kam 8 Ghante tak rakha hua paani labhkari hota hain. Jin logo ko cough ki samsya  jyada rahti hain, Unhe is paani mein Tulsi ke kuch patte daal dene chahiye. Bahut kam log jante hain ki Tambe ke bartan ka paani peene ke bahut saare fayde hain.
