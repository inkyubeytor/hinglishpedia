Fiber ki mauzoodagi pet ki bimariyo ko door rakhti hain aur pet ko aaram deti hain aur khane ko behatar tarike se pachaati hain. Yeh body ko healthy rakhne mein bahut hi upyogi hain, Kyonki yeh sharir se kai tarah ke zehareele padartho ko bahar nikaalne mein madad karti hain. Wajan kam karne mein madadgar :- In Black Eyed Pea mein Calorie kam hoti hain, Isliye yeh wajan kam karne ke liye accha aahaar hain. Lobia Dietary Fiber ka ek accha source hain, Jisse aapka pet jyada samay tak bhara rahta hain.  Healthy skin ke liye upyogi :- Skin ko suchaaroo roop se repair karte huye Lobia skin ko healthy rakhti hain. Is phalli mein mauzood Anti-oxidant mein Vitamin A aur C ki mauzoodgi hoti hain jo Free Radicals se skin ko huye nuksaan ko door karte hain.  Blood cholesterol level kam kare : - Black Eyed Pea blood cholesterol level ko kam karne ke liye jani jati hain aur kai tarah ki dil ki bimariyo ko door karti hain. Potassium aur Magnesium jaise Flavonoids aur Lignin ki mauzoodagi Lobia ko heart se related bimariyo ko door karne mein upyogi banati hain.  Diabetes ke mareezo ke liye faydemand :- Lobia ka glycemic index dusri istemal ki jane wali phaliyo aur daalo ke mukable kam hota hain.
