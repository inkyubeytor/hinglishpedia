Apach hone par pet bhari ho jata hain aur pet dard bhi karne lagta hain. Isse bechaini, jalan aur kabhi-kabhi mitali aane jaisi problems bhi ho sakti hain. Saath hi kuch logo ko khatti dakaar aur ulti jaisi shikayatein bhi hoti hain.
