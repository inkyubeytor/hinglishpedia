Aise mein Santre ka sewan karne se sharir mein paani toh milta hi hain, Saath hi yeh Fluid mein Sodium aur Potassium ki matra ko bhi balanced banaye rakhti hain.  5. Tension door karne mein madad karta hain :- Pregnancy mein Santra khane se tanaav door hota hain, Kyonki isme Potassium paya jata hain, Jiski wajah se Blood pressure control mein rahta hain.
