Puja karne ke liye kapoor ka istemal kiya jata hain. Lekin kya aapko pata hain ki Kapoor se jaha kai fayde hote hain, vanhi iske kuch nuksan bhi hain. Kapoor se hone wale nuksaan ke baare mein janiye. Kapoor se hone wale nuksan (Side-effects of Camphor in Hindi.) :-  1. Kapoor ke zyada istemal se aapko skin ulcer bhi ho sakta hain. 2. Kapoor pregnant ladies aur doodh pilaane wali maao ke liye nuksaandeh hoti hain. Yeh garbh mein pal bacche ko nuksaan pahuchaata hain. Isse bacche mein birth defect dekhne ko mil sakta hain. 3. Baccho ko kapoor zehar ki tarah hota hain. Agar aapke ghar mein chhote bacche hain to kapoor ko ghar mein khula bilkul bhi nahi chhodna chahiye. Agar galti se bacche ne kapoor ko nigal liya hain to aapko lene ke dene pad sakte hain. 4. Kapoor aapke pachan tantra ko nuksaan bhi pahucha sakta hain, Kyonki yeh aapke Gastrointestinal ko puri tarah se block bhi kar sakta hain. 5. Kapoor aapke sharir mein asaani se soak ho jata hain, Jisse aapko saans lene mein problem ho sakti hain. 6. Kapoor se nervous system aur kidney ko nuksaan pahuchta hain, Isliye agar aapko kidney ki bimari hain ya phir aap kidney ki bimari se bachna chahte hain to iska istemal na kare. 7. Agar aap Kapoor ko skin par direct apply karte hain to aapko skin allergy ya skin ki serious bimari ho sakti hain. Yaha tak ki aapko eczema bhi ho sakta hain.
